About the Trust

Anugya Kainkaryam Charitable Trust is founded to uphold the sacred tradition of Anugya Kainkaryam and Aagya Kainkaryam in our temples. Anugya Kainkaryam refers to the chanting of Veda parayanam, Divya Prabandham, and shlokas of Poorvacharyas on special and daily occasions in temples. Rooted in the ethos of “Sarveshameva Dharmanam Uthamo Vaishnavo Vithihi”, the trust honors the vision that rendering Bhagavad Aradhanam is the foremost dharma for all Vaishnavas.

Our services include recitation of Vedas and Divya Prabandhams, temple cleaning, making garlands, offering deepams, and more. The trust is committed to supporting and honoring those who render these divine services in Divyadesams and other temples of Emperumal.

“Rakshathe Bhagavan Vishnuhu: Bhaktanam Athamasareeravath”
A Vaishnavan rendering Bhagavad Aradhanam will always be in the vision of Emperumal and protected as His own.

Our Motive

Our aim is to gather Srivaishnavas for daily chanting of Vedam, Divya Prabandham, etc., in Divyadesams and temples, and to provide honorarium for their dedicated service. We also strive to support those who uphold the vaidheega tradition and foster a spiritually rich community.

Temples Served

We currently provide our services and sponsor inaugural Veda Parayanam and Divya Prabandham recitations at the following temples:

  1. Shri Yerikatha Raman, Shri Karunakara Perumal Thirukovil, Madhuranthakam
  2. Shri Aamaruviyappan Thirukovil, Therazhundhur
  3. Shri Aaravamudhan Thirukovil, Thirukudandhai
  4. Thiru Aadhanur Thirukovil
  5. Thirupullampoodhangudi Thirukovil
  6. Shri Veeraraghava Swamy Thirukovil, Thiruvallur
  7. Thiruvelliyankudi Thirukovil

Monthly funds are provided for Brahmins rendering Veda and Prabandham chanting at Madhuranthakam Sri Yerikatha Ramar Thirukovil. For other temples, amenities such as food, accommodation, and honorarium are provided during special occasions. Students learning Vedam and Prabandham are recognized with tests and rewards.

Other Activities & Social Service

  • Educational assistance for needy students
  • Caring for senior citizens
  • Helping widows for self-sustenance
  • Supporting farmers’ common needs
  • Tree plantation along roadsides
  • Reconstruction of village roads for better transportation
  • Furnishing and restoration of ancient temples in villages

Anugya Kainkaryam Charitable Trust is dedicated to serving the needs of our society and people.
